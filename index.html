Coire Walker

Coire Walker is an artist, music educator, and academic who applies divergent perspectives through an interdisciplinary skill set. A graduate of the ISS program at the University of Washington, his research connects history, economics, political science, and sociology, exploring the social forces affecting human belonging across Eastern and Western cultures. Coire aims to apply his professional and educational background and passion for urban environments to address complex societal problems. As an artist, researcher, and entrepreneur, he is uniquely positioned to contribute.
